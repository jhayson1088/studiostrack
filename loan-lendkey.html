Discover the LendKey Advantage LendKey operates as a bridge between consumers and community lenders, striving to establish a platform that offers the most transparent, accessible, and cost-effective borrowing solutions within the realm of online lending.

Amidst various lending options, students and parents are drawn to LendKey for three compelling reasons:

  • Inclusive Underwriting Approach In essence, LendKey leverages the strength of community lenders, including not-for-profit credit unions and community banks, to enhance the likelihood of loan approval. These community lenders boast a more favorable cost of funds compared to commercial banks and other online lenders. Consequently, they can extend these savings to borrowers by offering lower interest rates and a higher approval rate. Transparency & Choice Equipping students with comprehensive knowledge about their borrowing decisions is paramount. LendKey takes this commitment seriously, not only by offering financial education resources and guidance but also by developing interactive tools that illustrate how each choice influences the loan. Representing numerous community lenders across the nation, LendKey empowers students and families to select the loan that aligns with their needs. Exceptional Customer Service While community lenders fund each loan through LendKey, the platform establishes enduring connections with borrowers by providing unrivaled customer service. An internal survey reveals that 97% of LendKey borrowers express satisfaction with their loan experience (based on over 1,000 responses). Borrowers are treated as valued individuals, not mere leads, and are supported throughout the origination process and the loan's lifecycle. The same dedicated customer service representatives who assist borrowers during application also remain available for inquiries until the loan is fully repaid.
